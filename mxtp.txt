MXTP - Mixtape CLI Tools

Purpose: MXTP is a command-line tool to prepare mixtapes 
for cassette recording with maximum quality. It helps you:

- Minimize distortion
- Optimize cassette space
- Calculate total playback time
- Preview and organize track distribution between Side A and Side B

Important notes:

- The order of commands is fixed: TRIM → NORMALIZE → REORGANIZE.
- For safety, original files are cloned by default rather than overwritten.

Commands

TRIM
Removes leading and trailing silence from audio tracks, 
reducing unnecessary gaps and saving space on the cassette.

NORMALIZE
Adjusts the volume of all tracks to a consistent level, 
ensuring smooth playback without sudden jumps in loudness.

REORGANIZE
Distributes tracks between Side A and Side B to optimize cassette usage, 
automatically balancing the sides for minimal empty space.
