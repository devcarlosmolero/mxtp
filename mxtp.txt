MXTP - Mixtape CLI Tools

Purpose:
This CLI helps you record mixtapes on cassette with maximum quality by:
- Minimizing distortion
- Saving space
- Calculating total playback time
- Previewing track distribution between Side A and Side B

Important notes:
- The order of commands is fixed and cannot be changed:
  DURATION → SHUFFLE → TRIMMING → NORMALIZE → PREVIEW
- For safety, files are cloned by default instead of being replaced.
- To enable direct replacement of original files, use the --replace flag.

Commands:

DURATION
  Calculates the total playback time of all tracks in the selected folder.
  Ensures the mixtape fits within the cassette length.

SHUFFLE
  Randomizes the order of tracks before recording.

TRIMMING
  Removes leading and trailing silence from audio tracks.

NORMALIZE
  Adjusts audio levels to achieve consistent volume across all tracks.

PREVIEW
  Simulates how tracks will be distributed between Side A and Side B.
